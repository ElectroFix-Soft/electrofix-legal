Privacy Policy for ElectroFix

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

ElectroFix ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

  • Sensor Data (Local Processing): ElectroFix accesses high-sampling rate sensors (accelerometer) and battery statistics solely to optimize system performance through metabolic fingerprinting and drift calibration. This specific sensor data is processed locally on your device in real-time. We do not transmit sensor waveforms to external servers.
  • Device Identifiers (Cloud & Security): To prevent trial abuse, unauthorized tampering, and to ensure the integrity of the licensing system, we collect a unique Device ID (Android ID) and the Installation Timestamp. This data is stored securely using Google Firebase Realtime Database. This information is used strictly for App Functionality and Fraud Prevention.

2. Permissions

The application requests specific permissions strictly to maintain functionality and security:

  • WAKE_LOCK & FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required to maintain the performance shield active while the screen is off.
  • HIGH_SAMPLING_RATE_SENSORS: Required for precise hardware monitoring and calibration.
  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to verify the integrity of the trial period via our secure time servers (NTP) and secure cloud synchronization with Firebase.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

All financial transactions and subscriptions are handled securely through the Google Play Billing System. ElectroFix does not have access to, nor does it store, your credit card details or billing information. We only receive a cryptographic confirmation of an active subscription from Google Play to enable premium features.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to ensure the security and stability of the application:

  • Google Play Services: For application updates, security, and licensing verification.
  • Google Firebase: For secure data synchronization regarding trial status and fraud prevention.

These services may collect information used to identify your device in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain the minimal data required (Device ID and Trial Start Date) indefinitely. This is necessary to prevent users from bypassing the trial period limits by reinstalling the application (Anti-Fraud). This constitutes a legitimate interest for intellectual property protection.

6. Compliance

ElectroFix is designed to be compliant with global privacy standards (including GDPR and CCPA regarding Fraud Prevention exceptions). Users have full control over the application's activity and can stop the performance engine at any time via the user interface.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via our developer profile details on the Google Play Store.
