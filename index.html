Privacy Policy for ElectroFix

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

ElectroFix ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Sensor Data: ElectroFix accesses high-sampling rate sensors (accelerometer) and battery statistics solely to optimize system performance through metabolic fingerprinting and drift calibration. This data is processed locally on your device in real-time. We do not collect, store, or transmit any biometric or sensor data to external servers.

2. Permissions

The application requests specific permissions (WAKE_LOCK, HIGH_SAMPLING_RATE_SENSORS, FOREGROUND_SERVICE) strictly to maintain the performance shield and monitor hardware status during operation. No personal identification data is accessed via these permissions.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

All financial transactions and subscriptions are handled securely through the Google Play Billing System. ElectroFix does not have access to or store your credit card details or billing information. We only receive confirmation of an active subscription from Google Play to enable premium features.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Play Services to manage application updates and licensing. These services may collect information used to identify you in accordance with Google's own privacy policies.

5. Compliance

ElectroFix is designed to be compliant with global privacy standards, including GDPR. Users have full control over the application's activity and can stop the performance engine at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via our developer profile on the Google Play Store.
